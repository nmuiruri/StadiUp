About this website

This year I got a chance to plan and execute my own project of my choice. For this project I chose to create an attractive but informative website that helped students have effective and efficient study sessions. While researching about different study methods I conducted a survey as a primary source and personal addition to the research in which I compare the current study methods of my school's 9th graders and 7th graders observing the changes in study intensity and methods. From the results of the survey I was able to realize how important it is to teach the younger middle schoolers how to study properly to help maximize their academic performance.
